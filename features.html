2018 Toyota RAV4 Features

Engine & Performance

Powered by a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, delivering a balance of performance and efficiency.

Safety

  • Dynamic Radar Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist
  • Road Sign Assist

Comfort

Smooth and quiet ride with well-cushioned seats and dual-zone automatic climate control.

Technology

  • 7" touchscreen infotainment system
  • Navigation system
  • Premium JBL ® audio system
  • Bluetooth, USB, and voice recognition

Cargo Space

One of the largest in its class, with fold-flat rear seats expanding usable space significantly.

Exterior Design

Bold and aggressive styling with a sporty front grille and sleek lines.

All-Wheel Drive

Equipped with an advanced all-wheel-drive system for enhanced traction and stability in various driving conditions.

LED Lighting

Stylish and energy-efficient LED headlights and taillights for improved visibility and modern aesthetics.

Roof Rack

Integrated roof rails for additional cargo-carrying capacity, perfect for outdoor adventures.
