2018 Toyota RAV4
Features Media

Overview

The 2018 Toyota RAV4 Limited is a compact SUV that combines reliability, safety, and comfort. With a spacious interior and user-friendly technology, it is an ideal choice for families and individuals seeking a practical yet refined ride. Whether used for daily commutes or road trips, the RAV4 Limited provides a smooth driving experience and solid fuel economy, reinforcing Toyota’s strong reputation for dependable vehicles.

Reviews

“The 2018 RAV4 has excellent ride, comfort, and safety technology. I was ambivalent about getting the advanced technology package with parking sensors, surround cameras, front collision avoidance, and lane change detector, but now that I have it, very glad I did. Considered purchasing a Honda CRV or Subaru, but the RAV has better features and comfort.”

-- Emma (Source: Edmunds Reviews)

“Among the numerous compact-SUV choices out there, the 2018 Toyota RAV4 remains a standout. As the vehicle that started the compact-crossover-SUV segment, the RAV4 has consistently been a top seller, right alongside the Honda CR-V, Ford Escape and Subaru Forester. While not as off-road capable as a Jeep Cherokee, the RAV4 is far more reliable and holds exceptionally strong resale values. Its 4-cylinder engine delivers decent power with good fuel economy, and it has enough ground clearance to tackle most snow and light off-road situations. Better still, every RAV4 comes standard with Toyota’s Safety Sense-P suite of driver assists, and there’s even a hybrid model. Unfortunately, Toyota’s 2018 RAV4 still isn’t compatible with either Android Auto or Apple CarPlay.”

-- Joe Tralongo (Source: Kelley Blue Book)

“2018 Rav4 was totaled by someone who ran a stop sign. Miss this car every day. Every single airbag went off and me and the other passenger were safe and able to walk away alive. Thankful for this car.”

-- Jocelyn B (Source: Cars.com Reviews)
